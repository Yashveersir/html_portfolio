About Me

My name is Yashveer Singh, and I am currently pursuing my Bachelor of Engineering degree at the University Institute of Technology, The University of Burdwan. Hailing from Muzaffarpur, Bihar, I have always been driven by a passion for technology and a desire to create impactful solutions.

Currently, I reside in the AJC Bose Boys Hostel, where I engage with fellow students and immerse myself in my studies. In addition to my academic pursuits, I enjoy playing kabaddi, which helps me stay active and develop teamwork skills.

Looking ahead, I aspire to become a full-stack web developer, specializing in the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js). I am excited about the endless possibilities in web development and am eager to contribute to innovative projects that make a difference.
